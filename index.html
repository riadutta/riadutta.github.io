Welcome! I study questions in Development and Political Economy applying deep learning and structural methods to extract economic insights from unstructured historical and spatial data.

In the past, I have worked at LEAD at Krea University, J-PAL South Asia and IMAGO Global Grassroots. I hold an MA in Public Policy and Governance from Azim Premji University, and a BA in Economics and History from Tata Institute of Social Sciences
