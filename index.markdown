Hello! I study questions in Development and Political Economy, with a particular focus on gender and governance.

Recently, I led a field experiment to study the impact of peer networks among locally elected politicians in Bihar under the guidance of 
<a href="https://www.mrsharan.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Professor M.R. Sharan</a>.

Previously, I worked as a Senior Research Associate at 
<a href="https://imagogg.org/imago-india/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">IMAGO Global Grassroots</a> 
with 
<a href="https://www.hks.harvard.edu/faculty/michael-walton" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Professor Michael Walton</a>, 
evaluating the effectiveness of the national anti-poverty program (MGNREGA) on rural women’s collectives using quasi-experimental methods.

At 
<a href="https://www.povertyactionlab.org/south-asia" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">J-PAL South Asia</a>, 
I assessed the outcomes of a gender-inclusive curriculum on adolescent boys and girls with 
<a href="https://seemajayachandran.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Professor Seema Jayachandran</a>.

Before that, at 
<a href="https://ifmrlead.org/#/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">IFMR LEAD</a>, 
I studied the financial challenges faced by rural women entrepreneurs using primary data and helped develop an innovative credit scoring model as an alternative to traditional banking systems.

I also serve as Director of 
<a href="https://www.womenineconpolicy.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Women in Economics and Policy</a>, 
a non-profit organization dedicated to advancing women’s roles in economics and allied fields.
